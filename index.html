Privacy Policy

Essential Words AI — English Vocabulary Learning App

Last updated: June 8, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how the Essential Words AI mobile application ("Essential", "we", "us", or "our") collects, uses, and protects your information when you use the app. By using Essential, you agree to the practices described in this policy.

Information We Collect

We collect only the information needed to provide and improve the app:

  • Account information. When you sign in with Google or Apple, we receive your name, email address, and (for Google) profile picture, along with a unique account identifier. Signing in is optional and only required for features such as saving favorites, quizzes, and learning progress.
  • Learning data. Your study activity — daily streaks, completed units and books, saved (favorited) words, and comments you add — so your progress can be saved and synced.
  • Device & notification data. A push notification token (Firebase Cloud Messaging), your device platform (iOS/Android), and time zone, used to deliver streak reminders.

We do not collect your contacts, photos, precise location, or payment information.

How We Use Your Information

  • Authenticate your account and keep you signed in.
  • Save and sync your learning progress across sessions.
  • Send optional reminder notifications to help you keep your streak.
  • Operate, maintain, and improve the app.

We do not sell or rent your personal information to anyone, and we do not use it for third-party advertising.

Third-Party Services

Essential relies on a few trusted providers that may process limited data on our behalf:

  • Google Sign-In & Apple Sign in — for secure authentication.
  • Firebase Cloud Messaging (Google) — for push notifications.
  • Application hosting — our backend server stores your account and learning data.

These services have their own privacy policies governing how they handle data.

Data Retention & Account Deletion

We keep your data only while your account is active. You can permanently delete your account and all associated data at any time directly in the app:

  • Open Profile / Settings → Delete account.

Once confirmed, your profile, streaks, completed units, favorites, and registered notification devices are permanently removed from our servers. This action cannot be undone.

Push Notifications

With your permission, we send reminder notifications. You can turn these off any time from your device settings or via the Reminders toggle in the app, and the notification token is removed when you sign out or delete your account.

Security

We use industry-standard measures to protect your data, including encrypted connections (HTTPS) and token-based authentication. However, no method of transmission or storage is completely secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

Children's Privacy

Essential is intended for general audiences and is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided us with personal data, please contact us and we will remove it.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated "Last updated" date.
