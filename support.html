Frequently asked questions

How does Essential Words AI work?

The app is organized into 6 books and 180 units. Each unit has a short story, a list of words with definitions and audio pronunciation, and a quiz to test what you learned. Your progress is saved as you go.

How do I sign in?

You can sign in with your Google or Apple account. Your progress and saved words are linked to your account so you can continue on any device.

Is the audio pronunciation real?

Word pronunciation is generated using text-to-speech so you can hear how each word sounds clearly and consistently.

I'm not receiving reminder notifications.

Make sure notifications are enabled for Essential Words AI in your device Settings, and that you are signed in. If the problem continues, contact us with your device model.

How do I delete my account?

You can delete your account instantly from Profile / Settings → Delete account inside the app, or request deletion by email. See our Account Deletion page for full details.

Is my data private?

Yes. We only collect what's needed to run the app. Read our Privacy Policy to learn more.
